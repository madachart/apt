Where we are?

The apartment is located in the heart of the city center, within walking distance of the city’s
main attractions. A few examples: Danube bank, Chain bridge: 900 m, Parliament: 1.9 km,
Buda castle: 2 km, Opera house: 900 m, Academy of Music: 1 km, Rudas spa: 1.5 km,
Heroes' Square, City park: 3 km.
The apartment offers beautiful day and night views of the city, e.g. Gellért Hill, Deák Square
and Buda Hills.

Equipment

The apartment is stylishly furnished with modern appliances and is suitable for even longer
stays. Ideal: for four or 4 + 1 people.
A bedroom with a walk-in closet, a huge living room with a double sofa bed, a modern
kitchen and plenty of windows to shine, all with three French balconies it serves to make
guests feel comfortable
Facilities: new elevator, air conditioners, cable TV, wifi, dishwasher, fridge, washing machine, dryer, hair dryer, iron, baby coat, TV (100+ channels), DVD, iDock, fully equipped modern kitchen.

How you can get to
the apartment?

1. From the airport: airport bus or taxi
2. From Keleti railway station: to Deák tér by M2 then 5 minutes walk
3. From Nyugati railway station: to Deák tér by M3 then 5 minutes walk
4. From Kelenföld railway station: take the M4 to Kálvin tér, then the M3 to Deák tér and 5 minutes walk
5. by car: GPS coordinates: 47.497096, 19.057133
